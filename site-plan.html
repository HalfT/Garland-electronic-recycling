Overview

Purpose

Here at Garland Recycling we do our best to strengthen the community by recycling and repurposing unwanted electronics. Recycling helps reduce waste in the environment and can help schools receive needed supplies. I have worked for a school district for over 10+ years repairing all types of electronics for classrooms. I know the ins and outs of computers and how to keep your data safe. Repurposing and Recycling passions. At Garland Recycling we are a Certified Electronics Recycler here to serve those in our local community. We care about our envitement and safty and look forward to helping everyone get the most of our world's natural resources.

Audience

Have you ever had old electronics that you want to dispose of properly? That is what Garland Recycling is for. If a buisness or person needs these properly disposed of we can do that for them. Depending on the item we can even give cash back for the precious metals that are in these devices.
